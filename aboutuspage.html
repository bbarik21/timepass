Lifecare Hospital,Navi Mumbai

Life Care Hospital is recognized as one of the major hospitals in newly developed center of the city, Panvel, and is designed to provide new concept and services to the people of the city. Life Care hospital has come up with a new concept of Health Care with Love and so it has got a very devoted and concerned staff which helps the patients recover with utmost care. Now, talking about the quality, Lifecare Hospital is an ultra-modern hospital with third generation equipments and infrastructure, which gives accurate results and a right way to cure problems; highly skilled, qualified and motivated team of doctors with absolute hygiene care and cleanliness assurance.

Lifecare Hospital is a part of IHH Healthcare, one of the world’s largest healthcare providers. With its full spectrum of integrated services, dedicated people, reach and scale, and commitment to quality and safety, IHH aspires to be the world’s most trusted healthcare services network, united by a single purpose: to touch lives and transform care.

Clinical excellence has earned the hospital tie-ups with all major cashless health insurance players and being the preferred choice hospital by all major corporates in the city.

Accreditations


National Accreditation Board for Hospital and Healthcare Providers (NABH) Accreditation, India


Lifecare Hospital, Navi Mumbai, has attained NABH accreditation. NABH (accredited by ISQua, International Society for Quality in Healthcare) has been set up to bring the world's best healthcare quality standards to India by focusing on patient care &, safety, and continuous quality improvement. The hospital is committed to establish a culture of quality, patient safety, efficiency and accountability - it is protocol-driven and reflects global practices.




National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) Accreditation, India


Lifecare Hospital, Navi Mumbai's Medical Laboratories are accredited by NABL, India’s leading accreditation body for medical laboratories. Our team of highly experienced and competent staff, well-organized sample management practices and robust quality management programme ensure accurate and precise results.
